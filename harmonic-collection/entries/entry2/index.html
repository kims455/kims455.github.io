How It All Began

This journey of finding extraordinary and intriguing things around me aims to make my days even more special and exhilarating, adding some fantasy and stories to the things around me. It all started in my childhood. My parents always told me to ask questions whenever they came up in my head to develop my knowledge, imagination, and creativity. I never stopped asking them questions; they always gave me the answer or tried to find the answer with me so I could learn and experience the process of finding the best answers to my questions. These moments made me develop into a more particular person with a unique imagination and curiosity.

Interests and curiosities come to my mind like it's snowing. It falls all over the place, and it is my job to look at each of them and pick the best for me. My ideas are connected, just like making snowballs. Then, I gather my interests, ideas, and inspirations to make what I want. Everything and everyone around me gives ideas. Indeed, I like to focus on small details around me to find something intriguing or unexpected. I write those details and ideas related to those in my notes to remember and expand or combine with other ideas to create what I want. Moreover, I like to walk and look at what is around me. While walking, I listen to music, which makes me dive deep into my thoughts to ask questions and ideas.

Furthermore, the environment is also essential when I discover unexpected things where I get ideas. I remember getting different inspirations and interests when I lived in Irvine, California, and Seoul, South Korea. I enjoy reading the journals and essays I wrote in those two cities, and they are different in many areas, such as emotion, weather, people I met, friendships, transportation, perspective, and goals. Those precious memories and new experiences bring fresh ideas that excite me. And now, in NYC, I am starting a new chapter of my discovery journal.
