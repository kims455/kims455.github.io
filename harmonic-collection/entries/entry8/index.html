Post-it notes





From To-Do Lists to Bright Ideas

Since middle school, post-it notes have been the best tool to keep track of the tasks and reminders I need to complete, turning the ordinary into something extraordinary. Those small, colorful squares have helped me become a well-organized and planned person. Nowadays, I often use the apps on my phone to limit the amount of paper waste. From to-do lists to bright ideas, post-it notes were like canvases for my thoughts and brainstorm all the ideas to create works.
