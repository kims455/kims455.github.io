A Little Joy in Everyday Life

Welcome to the the journey of finding little joy in our daily lives!

"A Little Joy in Everyday Life" is a journey that seeks to unveil the hidden sides of NYC's identity by capturing distortions around us.

I will explore the multifaceted and surreal aspects of daily life in NYC.

I will show a dynamic, unusual, and thought-provoking perspective on urban existence where reality and illusion seamlessly intermingle.

I will find intriguing and interesting moments of in my daily life each week.
